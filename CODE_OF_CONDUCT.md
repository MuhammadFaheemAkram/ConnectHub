# Code Of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We want ConnectHub to be a welcoming, respectful project for Android developers of all backgrounds and experience levels.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Keep discussions focused on the project.
- Welcome newcomers and help them understand the codebase.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Discriminatory language or behavior.
- Public or private intimidation.
- Spam or deliberately disruptive comments.
- Sharing private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or block contributors whose behavior is harmful to the community.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns through the security/contact process in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Include relevant links, screenshots, or context when possible.
